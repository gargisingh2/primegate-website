What the Partnership Gives You

Five Reasons This Partnership Is Different From the Rest.

Primegate's university partnerships have been built over thirty years of personal engagement - with international recruitment directors, regional coordinators, and admissions offices that trust the Primegate name before a student application arrives. As a channel partner, you carry that institutional credibility into every student conversation. That is an access advantage no competitor network can match in this region.

Primegate operates on a fully disclosed commission model - competitive with the market, paid promptly upon enrolment confirmation, and never contingent on volume targets that compromise advisory quality. We believe agents who are paid fairly advise students better. Our commission structure reflects that belief.

The Primegate partner onboarding programme is not a product catalogue. It is an immersion into the institutional knowledge, market intelligence, and advisory methodology that has produced results for three decades. Partner agents leave onboarding knowing more about the universities they represent - their admissions culture, cohort priorities, and scholarship frameworks - than most agent networks learn in years.

In the education advisory market, brand association matters. Families that recognise the Primegate name bring a level of trust and seriousness to the advisory relationship that generic agent interactions rarely command. Our partners benefit from that brand equity - in every meeting, every referral, and every application submitted under the Primegate umbrella.

The ethical and regulatory landscape of international student recruitment is increasingly scrutinised - by universities, regulators, and families. Primegate's compliance framework, documentation standards, and institutional relationship protocols are designed to ensure that every partner operates within the highest ethical standards. This protects your business, your reputation, and the students you serve.

Eligibility & Selectivity

Who Primegate Chooses to Partner With.

The Primegate partner network is not open registration. Every partner is reviewed, assessed, and onboarded individually - because the quality of every student Primegate places is a direct function of the quality of the advisory that precedes the placement.

We are looking for agents and consultancies that share our belief in honest, student-centred advising - not operators who are primarily interested in volume.

  • A minimum of two years of active education advisory practice, or equivalent professional background
  • An established student base or referral network in at least one core source market (GCC, South Asia, SEA, CIS, Africa)
  • A demonstrable understanding of the international higher education application process for at least one destination market
  • No documented history of non-compliance, misrepresentation, or regulatory sanction
  • Genuine alignment with Primegate's values: student-first guidance, full transparency, and ethical commission practices

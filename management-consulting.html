Why a Free Zone

The Structure Most International Founders Start With.

For founders entering the UAE from abroad, a free zone company is often the fastest, cleanest route to a fully owned, fully compliant business. It is built for international trade, services, consulting and holding structures - and it can be operational in days, not months.

Primegate advises on the trade-off honestly. A free zone is ideal for many businesses; a mainland licence is the right answer for others. We recommend the structure that fits how you actually intend to operate - not the one that is easiest to sell.

  • 100% foreign ownership - no local sponsor required
  • 0% corporate tax on qualifying free zone income
  • Full repatriation of capital and profits
  • Customs duty benefits on qualifying activity
  • Trade licence issued in as little as 48 hours
  • Choice of IFZA, Meydan, DMCC and RAKEZ
